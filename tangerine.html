2017/2018
Tangerine

Home / Portfolio

Hired as the Marketing Department's dedicated Front-end Developer during the redevelopment of Tangerine’s website. Beyond setting up development, design and QA processes and suggesting tools/software for the team I was tasked to manage, my responsibilities included creating wireframes by extending on existing designs and consequently developing modularized and sustainable front-end components using Sass/CSS, HTML and AngularJS.

Post launch of the redesigned platform, I was promoted to Senior Front-end Developer, where my onus shifted from day-to-day tasks to more large-scale projects, while still acting as a team lead for the other Front-end Developers. Such projects included leading the SEO iniative, streamlining the development process with a focus on making the team more "agile," working closely with the Data Analytics team to correct and maintain tracking on the site, implementing Accessibility standards in order to be WCAG 2.0 AA compliant, and setting up A/B tests through Google Optimize.
